Our goal at AEU is to provide quality education online through rigorous coursework and work assessments. We have been able to graduate over 3000 students. Our strengths has always been our flexibility and the ability to give personal education through an online platform.

We have spent many years developing our online program. Our team of career academic advisers and help-desk support is vested in our student's success. Through AEU, students will have access to support services just like students on campuses.

Universitas Indonesia is one of the premiere universities in Indonesia. They have decided to merge with us and will incorporate our program under their banner. We are extremely excited about this as it will allow us to grow and help more students
